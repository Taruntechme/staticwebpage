Troubleshooting
A Troubleshootin is just to analyse and solve serious problems for a company or other organization Troubleshooting is just a form of problem solving, often applied to repair failed products or processes on a system. It is a logical, systematic search for the source of a problem in order to solve it, and make the product or process operational again. Troubleshooting is needed to identify the symptoms. Determining the most likely cause is a process of elimination‚Äîeliminating potential causes of a problem. Finally, troubleshooting requires confirmation that the solution restores the product or process to its working state.

A system can be described in terms of its expected, desired or intended behavior. Events or inputs to the system are expected to generate specific results or outputs.Any unexpected or undesirable behavior is a symptom. Troubleshooting is the process of isolating the specific cause or causes of the symptom. Frequently the symptom is a failure of the product.Any action that can be taken to prevent from failures.
